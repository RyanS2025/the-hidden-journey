The Emotional Centerpiece

Olivia Chaffin's Story

"

Olivia Chaffin was a Girl Scout in Tennessee when she decided to look more closely at the label on her cookie box.

The palm oil in her cookies carried a "mixed" certification label — meaning it could come from sustainable sources, or from plantations linked to deforestation and labor abuses. There was no way to tell which.

She didn't look away. She researched. She asked questions the adults around her couldn't answer. And then she launched a petition that got national attention, challenging one of the most trusted organizations in America to do better.

"One Girl Scout asked a question.
That question became a movement."
